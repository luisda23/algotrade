FAQ

Got questions?

The most common ones we get.

Do I need to know how to code?
No, not at all. The wizard walks you through step by step. You pick market, pair, strategy and indicators with clicks. We generate the code for you.
Does it work with any broker?
Yes — any broker that supports MetaTrader 4 or 5. That's most of them: IC Markets, Pepperstone, OANDA, XM, FTMO, etc.
Is it compatible with FTMO and other prop firms?
Absolutely. When you build the bot, you can pick your prop firm from 12 supported ones (FTMO, FundedNext, The5ers, E8 Markets, Funding Pips, City Traders Imperium and more). The wizard tells you which firms allow EAs and what rules to respect so you don't lose your funded account.
Do I need my PC turned on all the time?
If you want the bot to trade 24/7, MetaTrader has to stay open. The simplest setup is renting a VPS (~$10/month) so your bot runs all the time without using your electricity.
Are profits guaranteed?
No. Trading involves risk of loss. We always recommend testing the bot in a DEMO account first, then starting with small capital on a real account until you've validated the results.
Can I edit the bot later?
Yes. Parameters (Stop Loss, Take Profit, lot size, trading hours) can be tweaked from MetaTrader itself. If you want a completely different strategy, build another bot from the wizard.
Are refunds available?
If the file doesn't work or you have technical issues we can't solve within 7 days, you get a full refund. Other cases are reviewed individually. Email us at support@yudbot.com.
